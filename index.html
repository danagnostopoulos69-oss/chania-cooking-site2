About Us

We are two professional private chefs based in Chania, Crete, specializing in high-end, tailor-made dining experiences for villas, private residences and exclusive occasions.

Our culinary journey includes participation in MasterChef Greece (seasons 7 and 9), an experience that refined our creativity, technical precision and commitment to excellence.

We combine authentic Greek flavors with modern Mediterranean techniques, focusing on premium seasonal ingredients, refined presentation and balanced menus.

From menu design and sourcing to cooking and full kitchen clean-up, we manage every detail so our guests enjoy a seamless, stress-free experience.
